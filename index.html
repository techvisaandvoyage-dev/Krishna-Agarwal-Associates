Krishna Agarwal & Associates is a Chartered Accountancy firm committed to providing professional services in the areas of taxation, audit and assurance, accounting, GST compliance, corporate compliance, and business advisory.

Founded with a vision to deliver reliable and practical financial solutions, the firm works closely with businesses, startups, professionals, and individual clients to help them meet their regulatory requirements.

We believe in building long-term relationships through integrity and a client-centric approach. Our focus is on understanding each client's unique requirements and providing timely, effective solutions.

Our services include Income Tax Advisory and Compliance, GST Services, Audit and Assurance, Accounting and Bookkeeping, Company Incorporation and ROC Compliance, and Business Advisory.

At Krishna Agarwal & Associates, we strive to uphold the highest standards of professional ethics while delivering quality services that add value to our clients' businesses and financial well-being.
